About us

Built to carry more of the load.

Proware Associates is a fully registered cargo and logistics company serving businesses and individuals moving goods across the UK and internationally.

We grew out of years of hands-on experience in cargo and logistics, and built Proware around a simple idea: clients shouldn't have to stitch together a shipper, a leasing firm and a warehouse. We bring all three together under one roof — and one relationship.

Whether you're sending a single shipment, leasing capacity for a season, or storing and fulfilling stock long-term, we handle it with the care and accountability of a partner, not just a courier.
